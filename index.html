Quiz Website

A quiz is a brief assessment or competition designed to test someone's knowledge, understanding, or skills on a particular subject or range of topics. Typically consisting of a series of questions, quizzes can be educational, entertaining, or competitive in nature. They serve as effective tools for learning, evaluation, and engagement, often found in classrooms, online platforms, and social gatherings.

Quiz Guide

1.Introduction: Start with a brief introduction to the quiz topic and format. 2.Rules: Clearly outline the rules and expectations for participants. 3.Scoring: Describe how scoring will be handled and when results will be provided. 4.Behavior: Remind participants of expected etiquette and behavior during the quiz. 5.At the end every participants get their score.
